Three Word Story

The worldwide collaboration

How does it work?

The idea is to build a story that everyone collaborates to and it's based on the popular internet forum game Three Word Story.

Once you have entered your three words you have done your bit in contributing to the story!

When the story is finished it will either end up hilarious or unreadable, that's the fun part!

There is a live preview button in the top corner of the page, so you can keep up with how the story is unfolding so far.
